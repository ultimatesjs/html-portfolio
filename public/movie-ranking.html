The Best Movies According to Simranjeet

My Top 3 movies of all-time.

I-Robot

I-Robot is a thought-provoking sci-fi thriller that explores the question of whether robots can be truly intelligent and moral.

Sweyed Away

Spirited Away is a visually stunning and heartwarming coming-of-age story about a young girl who learns the importance of self-reliance and courage.

Border

Border is a must-see for anyone who loves patriotic movies or war films.
